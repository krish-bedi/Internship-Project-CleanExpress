About Clean Express
Clean Express provides high quality laundry and dry cleaning services for your everyday wear. It has a business model that combines servicing laundry requirements for Enterprise and individual consumers (residential & Communities).It is fully committed for providing professional and superior quality laundry services that will deliver satisfaction and delight to our customers every day.
Vision
To make our customers look their best and feel confident with crisp, and clean clothes everyday -to thereby add happiness and glitter to their lives.
Mission
To be the most widely patronized, respected and loved consumer brand in the laundry service business.
Environmental Policy
As a responsible corporate citizen, we handle & dispose off cleaning solutions in accordance with Government regulations. We use energy and water efficient machines, eco-friendly detergent, waste separation systems and energy saving schemes to reduce natural resource consumption and pollution.
